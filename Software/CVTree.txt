CVTree

CVTree constructs whole-genome based phylogenetic trees without sequence alignment by using a Composition Vector (CV) approach. 


Reference:
Ji Qi, Bin Wang, Bailin Hao (2004) Whole proteome prokaryote phylogeny without sequence alignment: a K-string composition approach, J Mol Evol, 58: 1 ¨C11
Guanghong Zuo, Bailin Hao (2015) CVTree3 web server for whole-genome-based and alignment-free prokaryotic phylogeny and taxonomy, Genomics Proteomics & Bioinformatics, 13: 321-331.


http://tlife.fudan.edu.cn/archaea/cvtree/cvtree3/
